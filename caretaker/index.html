🏠 Apartment Guide

🫧 Washing Machine
Select cold wash → choose water level → ignore cycle → choose Economy → Start. Put powder or capsule in with the washing.
🤖 Robot Vac
Watch that cables don't get caught in it. Best to empty it partway through if you're there when it's running — press the button to pause, empty, then resume.
🌡️ Heater
Feel free to use it — just don't forget to turn it off before you leave. Do not leave on if you're not home.
🚿 Shower
Put the extractor fan on when you use the shower.
🍽️ Dishwasher
  1. Put the dishwasher tablet in the left-hand side flap and close it
  2. Close the dishwasher door
  3. Press Power on
  4. Press P multiple times to scroll through to P4
  5. Press the ▷ triangle button to start
